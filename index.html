Incredible Mom attributes

Impossibly Strong

Despite everything standing in her way, she managed to get her associate degree and start an amazing career as a medical coder. She is a first generation college graduate who inpired her son to do the same.

Business Owner

My Mom is the proud owner of Julie's Bling Boutique, a business she runs even though she still works a full time job

Genuine Care and Love

My Mom is dedicated to her family and friends. Never have you felt a love as strong as my Mom's. If you are in her life my Mom will go to the ends of the earth for you and expect nothing in return. She is fiercely loyal and gives all her heart to the people in it.

Family

Despite working a full time job and running a business on the side Mom manages to keep this family organized and fed every day. The woman has so much on her plate you'd wonder how she does it. She'll tell you even she doesn't know, but the fact is simply, she cares.

Dedication

To further demonstrate how incredible my Mom is she was formerly a teacher at the local community college. She is so dedicated to her former students she not only stays in contact with them but goes out of her way to find them jobs. Amazing.

Get ready to meet an incredible person

No matter what this world throws at her she always comes out on top. My Mom is my best friend. She's stuck by me even through all the horrible things I've done and done to her. When God was picking out mother and son combinations he knew that this one was like peanut butter and jelly: Weird on paper but oh, so good.

If you're looking for an inspirational story, then look no further. My Mom has overcome all the odds to become the incredible woman she is today.
